2. **Watch the video** — hear the concept explained with examples
3. **Try the concept** — use a notebook from [EquityStack](https://github.com/Varnasr/EquityStack) or [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/) to apply it
4. **Review the definition** — check [key_concepts.md](key_concepts.md) for a concise reference
